About the Event

Twelve years of
bringing people together.

The Westside Gala has grown from a small gathering of 40 community members into the most anticipated annual event in the region. Every year we bring together the people shaping the future of business, culture, and community in the West Side and beyond.

This year we are expecting over 400 guests across an evening of keynote conversations, fine dining, live performances, and the annual Community Impact Awards.
