Why Ridgeline

What sets us apart from other Virginia roofing contractors.

01

No Subcontractors on Any Job

Every person on your roof is a trained, background-checked Ridgeline employee. Not a third party who was handed a phone call this morning.

02

One Fixed Price. No Surprises.

The number on your quote is the number on your invoice. We do not discover additional costs on installation day. Every quote is reviewed by a senior project manager before it leaves our office.

03

10-Year Workmanship Warranty

Every installation carries our own warranty on top of the manufacturer guarantee. If the roof leaks because of how it was installed within 10 years, we come back and fix it. No arguments.

04

Insurance Claim Specialists

We provide comprehensive documentation for storm damage claims including measurements, photographs, and technical reports in the format adjusters need to approve claims efficiently.

05

Honest Assessments

We have turned away replacement jobs because the roof only needed a repair. We would rather have a customer for life than oversell a job. Several homeowners have saved thousands because we told them the truth.

06

Clean Jobsites Every Time

We use magnetic nail rollers, tarps to protect landscaping, and a full walkthrough before we leave. Your property looks better after we finish than it did when we arrived.
